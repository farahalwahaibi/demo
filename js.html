The Cheesecake Shop story began in Amman, Jordan in the 2019’s. FARAH and TASNIM found a recipe in the local newspaper that would inspire them “Original” Cheesecake. Everyone loved their recipe so much that they decided to open a small Cheesecake shop, And because of Covid-19 they moved their baking equipment to a kitchen in their basement and continued to sup ply cakes to several of the best restaurants in Jordan.
